Protein oats

Description

Tasty dessert that is healthy as well.

Ingredients

  • 80g (preferably fine) oats
  • 280ml (preferably 1.5% fat) milk
  • 18g chocolate flavored protein powder
  • 3g cocoa

Steps

  1. Add the oats in a small bowl
  2. Pour the milk on it
  3. Microwave for 2 minutes
  4. Add the protein powder and cocoa mix
  5. Stir it all together
