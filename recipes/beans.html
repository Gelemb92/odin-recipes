Beans a'la Bud Spencer

Description

Beans like the ones Bud Spencer and Terence Hill had in their films.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoon oil
  • 150g bacon
  • 500g red onion
  • salt, pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoon tomato purée
  • 700-800g beans
  • 500ml water

Steps

  1. Slice the bacon in small pieces
  2. Toast the bacon in the oil
  3. Take out the toast and leave the oil in the pan
  4. Toast the sliced up onions in the oil until they areglossy
  5. Add the salt and pepper
  6. Add the sliced up garlic
  7. Toast it a little then add the tomato purée
  8. Add the beans and the previously toasted bacon
  9. Pour on the water
  10. Stir it and cook until it gets fairly thick
