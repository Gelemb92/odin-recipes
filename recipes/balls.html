Coconut Balls

Description

Delicious dessert which can be done quickly.

Ingredients

  • 50dkg ground biscuits
  • 4 tablespoon cocoa
  • 30dkg sugar
  • 2dl milk
  • 20dkg butter
  • 10dkg shredded coconut

Steps

  1. Add and stir the ground biscuits, the cocoa and the sugar in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter and add it to the rest alongside the milk
  3. Stir it up well and put in the fridge for at least half an hour
  4. Put the shredded coconut in another bowl
  5. Get the previous bowl out of the fridge and start making small balls from your mixture
  6. Roll the balls in the shredded coconut and put the finished ball on a plate
  7. Repeat until there is no more mixture left :)
